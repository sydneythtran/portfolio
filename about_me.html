About Me!

Hello! My name is Sydney and I am a Portland, OR based data scientist.

I am a committed team player who has hands-on experience solving complex machine learning problems and creating models by utilizing statistical analysis.

Combining data science with my past experiences allows me to have an innovative and multi-faceted view when analyzing data to discover customer preferences, identify trends, and solve business problems.

As an Asian-American, I want to be a change in a predominantly male-oriented field.
